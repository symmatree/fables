# WiFiman throughput / latency (Pixel 10 Pro)

## Before cable fix (spa-eaves uplink suspect)

**Front yard, S end of run**
- AP: spa-eaves-uap
- 5 GHz, 80 MHz width; WiFi 5 MIMO 3x3
- RSSI: -60 dBm
- 94.2 Mbps down, 230.5 Mbps up, 14 ms latency
- 3 ms to gateway

## After readopting spa-eaves AC and fixing broken cable (balcony to basement)

**2026-03-15.** WiFiman, Pixel 10 Pro in the yard.

- **In yard (good spot):** 486 Mbps down, 449 Mbps up, 21 ms latency. Channel 5, -51 dBm.
- **~End of run** (approx. same area as "before"): 272 Mbps down, 255 Mbps up, 20 ms latency.

Much better than the pre-fix numbers in this doc, but there is a lot of variation with position and angle, so it's hard to say how much was position vs the partially severed cable.
